## Legal Basis for Current Approach

The TTU policy explicitly states:
1. Restrictions apply to **"classroom instructional activities"** only
2. **"Student-directed work, clinical and research areas are not affected"**
3. Faculty **First Amendment rights in personal capacity** are recognized
4. Prior state law (SB 17) **exempted academic research and creative work**

This personal website showcasing published peer-reviewed research is therefore exempt. The changes above are **professional caution**, not legal compliance.
