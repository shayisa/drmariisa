Bridging Forensic Science & Social Justice

Dr. Mariyam Isa is a biological anthropologist specializing in forensic fractography and the identification of marginalized individuals. She offers expert commentary, consulting services, and speaking engagements on gender-informed forensic strategies and skeletal trauma analysis.
